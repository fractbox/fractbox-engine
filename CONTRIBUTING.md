# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in Fractbox Engine.

## This is a read-only mirror

The engine is developed in a private monorepo and published here **one-way**. As a
result:

- **Pull requests are automatically closed.** Not out of rudeness — there's simply
  no path for a PR against this mirror to flow back to the upstream source, so a
  merge here would be overwritten on the next publish. A bot closes PRs with a
  pointer to this file.
- **Issues are open and genuinely read.** Bug reports, rendering glitches, WebGPU
  compatibility problems, and "the math is wrong here" reports are all welcome and
  the most useful thing you can send. Please include your browser, OS, and GPU, and
  the formula/preset if relevant.
- **Ideas and discussion** are welcome in Issues too.

If you want to build on the engine, the MIT license lets you fork freely — that's
the intended path for derivative work.
